About Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a circular field. The objective of the game is to score more runs than the opposing team. A run is scored when a batsman hits the ball and runs between the wickets, while the opposing team attempts to field the ball and prevent runs.

Cricket has a rich history dating back to the 16th century, and it has evolved into various formats, including Test cricket, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20) matches.

Famous Cricketers

Throughout its history, cricket has seen numerous legends. Players like Sir Donald Bradman, Sachin Tendulkar, and Virat Kohli have left an indelible mark on the sport. Their skills and achievements continue to inspire new generations of cricketers.
